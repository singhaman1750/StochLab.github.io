### Prospective Graduate Students

If you are interested in working in our lab there are two ways to apply: <br/>
- Interdisciplinary Programmes for PhD/MTech/Mtech Research, which is offered by Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber Physical Systems at IISc.<br/>
- Other graduate programs in the department of associated faculties at IISc.

Please indicate CPS as one of your preferences while applying. [Apply Here](https://admissions.iisc.ac.in/)
